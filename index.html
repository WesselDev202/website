Cuber 2: Ice Age Remake

Cuber 2 Ice Age Remake is a challenging and addictive pixel art platformer game that will put your skills to the test. In this game, players must navigate through a hazardous ice-filled environment filled with dangerous enemies that they must avoid in order to reach the exit. The game features two control options to choose from: keyboard controls, which use A and D to move left and right, and W to jump, or controller controls, which use the left stick to move and A to jump. Before starting the game, players are prompted to press "Start New Game" to begin their icy adventure. Once the game starts, players must use their platforming skills to jump, slide and dodge their way through each level, avoiding deadly enemies and hazards along the way. The ultimate goal is to reach the exit at the end of each level, which will take players to the next challenging level. Cuber 2 Ice Age Remake's pixel art graphics give the game a retro and nostalgic feel, while the challenging gameplay mechanics make it an exciting and rewarding experience. If you're looking for a fun and challenging platformer game to sink your teeth into, then look no further than Cuber 2 Ice Age Remake!
